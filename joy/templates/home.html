JVF aims to transform the lives of young Kenyan men and women. In them we look to create a bright future for the country by instilling good morals, values and prosperity. We pursue this mission with zeal and commitment through faith and trust in God through a holistic and open approach.

The JVF movement started in central Kenya, in an area ravaged by the negative effects of illicit alcohol and drugs that had eaten up the fabrics of the society over many years.

A prosperous society has slowly slid into despair and destruction as a result of a vice that could have been easily managed.
